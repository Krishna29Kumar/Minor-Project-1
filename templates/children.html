InnerVoice – Emotional & Well-Being Check

This is anonymous. Answer honestly.

1. Have you spoken to a counsellor before?

2. Do school or college responsibilities feel overwhelming at times?

3. What part of academics feels hardest right now?

4. Do you sometimes feel worried, restless, or unable to relax?

5. Have there been days recently when you felt low?

6. When these feelings come up, how easy is it to manage?

7. Do relationships leave you confused or hurt?

8. Do you find it hard to express your feelings?

9. Is there someone you wish understood you better?

10. Do things at home affect your mood?

11. Do you feel heard by your family?

12. Is there something about your family situation you wish to talk about?

13. Do you feel anxious about your future?

14. Do you feel pressure to choose a path?

15. What worries you most?

16. How often do you feel confident?

17. Are you hard on yourself?

18. One thing you wish you felt confident about?

19. When stressed, what do you usually do?

20. Is there someone you feel safe talking to?

21. What support would help most?

22. What made you use this platform today?

23. What do you hope to gain from counselling?

24. Anything you're nervous about regarding counselling?
